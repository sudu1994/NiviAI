About

Built for India,
by people who understand it.

India's gig economy grew 38% in FY25 — the fastest in the world. 12 million Indians earn through gig and freelance work today, and 23.5 million will by 2030. The tools to help them find and validate the right idea are almost entirely built for the US market.

This platform changes that. Every data source is filtered for India. Every price is in rupees. Every strategy leads to platforms Indians actually use — Fiverr, Urban Company, Meesho, Instamojo. Every roadmap is built for someone running a side-hustle alongside a full-time job.

The result is a complete, sourced, actionable strategy in 10 seconds — not a generic answer but a real analysis with live data behind every recommendation.

All statistics sourced from India's Economic Survey 2025–26, NITI Aayog, NPCI, Grand View Research (2026) and TRAI. Scores are data-driven estimates — not financial advice. Individual results depend on effort and execution.
